Services

Making your digital services accessible, compliant, and built to last.

Who We Work With

We specialize in helping government agencies prepare for digital accessibility compliance. Whether you're preparing for the 2026 ADA Title II deadline or building a new digital service from scratch, we'll help you meet federal standards and serve your entire community - including the 1 in 4 U.S. adults with a disability.

From small town websites to large county portals, school districts to state agencies, public libraries to municipal services - we understand the unique challenges government faces and the compliance standards you need to meet.

Packages We Offer

Compliance Audit + Roadmap

Get a clear picture of exactly what needs to be fixed and a step-by-step plan to get there. We review your site using both manual and automated testing, then give you a clear scorecard and prioritized roadmap that shows what to fix first and how to meet WCAG 2.1 AA.

Audit + Remediation Guidance

Everything in the audit package, plus weekly support for your staff or vendors. We join your working sessions, answer questions, and review progress so you can be confident every change is moving you closer to compliance.

Full Program Build

Our complete accessibility transformation - audit, roadmaps, remediation support, built-in processes, procurement requirements, and verification testing. You'll get a complete accessibility policy and procedural framework after the project ends.

Compliance Partner Retainer

Keep your ongoing accessibility partner so you can stay compliant year after year. Quarterly scans, new content/vendor reviews, on-call consulting, and annual training refreshes so you never fall behind.

Inclusive Design Support

Accessibility doesn't have to wait until after a site launches. If you're planning a new service or redesign, we'll help you design it to be inclusive from the start. That includes workshops, project-specific guidance, and ongoing consultations that guide your team from concept to launch.

How We Work

Our approach is built on three core principles that ensure your long-term success:

Independent & Objective

We provide unbiased oversight and verification while your staff or vendors handle implementation. This creates an independent audit trail and helps your agency build the skills to maintain accessibility long-term.

Collaborative

We work alongside your developers, content creators, and stakeholders as an extension of your team. Through regular check-ins, plain-language checkpoints, and clear priorities so everyone knows exactly what to do.

Strategic & Sustainable

Our focus isn't just getting you compliant - it's keeping you compliant over time. We build accessibility into your processes and train your staff so you have the framework to stay with your team.
