What's Included in Every Package

  • Independent Oversight - We guide and verify while your team or vendors do the implementation work. This independence creates a defensible audit trail and protects you from vendor overselling or underdelivering.
  • Clear, Defensible Documentation - Plain-language reports and compliance records ready for leadership review or DOJ audits, demonstrating your good-faith compliance efforts.
  • Practical Guidance - We translate complex WCAG requirements into actionable steps that make sense for your team and your budget, even for non-technical staff.
  • Accessible Deliverables - All reports and documents provided in accessible PDF format—because we hold ourselves to the same standards we advocate for.
