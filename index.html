How We Help

Getting compliance doesn't have to feel overwhelming. We break it down into simple steps and support your team along the way.

  1. 1

    Step 1 - Independent Audit

    We test your website or portal using both manual and automated testing to identify accessibility barriers.

    • You'll get a clear picture of where things stand today.
  2. 2

    Step 2 - Roadmap to Compliance

    We prioritize what to fix first, estimate the work required, and create a plan to get it all done.

    • You'll have a step-by-step to-do list to reach compliance.
  3. 3

    Step 3 - Ongoing Guidance

    Every week, we meet with your staff or vendors to review progress, answer questions, and keep things on track.

    • You'll know exactly what progress is being made toward compliance.
  4. 4

    Step 4 - Training & Capability Building

    We build accessibility into your processes so you don't have to start over each time.

    • Your staff becomes confident and capable long after our project ends.
    • Optional: Ongoing support for systems, spot checks, and guidance so you never fall behind.
  5. 5

    Step 5 - Proactive Accessibility

    Accessibility doesn't have to be an afterthought. We can train staff members, review mockups, and provide ongoing support.

    • You'll never have reactive accessibility issues again.
