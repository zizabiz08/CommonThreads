How We Help

Getting compliant doesn't have to feel overwhelming. We break it down into simple steps and support your team along the way.

  1. 1

    Step 1 - Independent Audit

    We test your website or portal using both manual and automated testing to identify accessibility barriers.

    • You'll get a clear picture of where things stand today.
  2. 2

    Step 2 - Roadmap to Compliance

    We prioritize what to fix first, estimate the work required, and create a plan to get it all done.

    • You'll have a step-by-step to-do list tied to federal standards.
  3. 3

    Step 3 - Ongoing Guidance

    Each week, we meet with your staff or vendors to review progress, answer questions, and troubleshoot issues in real time.

    • You'll know every change is moving you closer to compliance.
  4. 4

    Step 4 - Training & Capability Building

    We build accessibility into your processes so you don't have to start over each time.

    • Your staff becomes confident and capable long after our project ends.
    • Optional: Keep us on retainer for quarterly scans, spot checks, and ongoing guidance so you'll never fall behind.
  5. 5

    Step 5 - Proactive Accessibility

    Accessibility doesn't have to be an afterthought. We can lead workshops, guide new projects, and provide human-centered design support so accessibility is built in from the very beginning.

    • You'll save time, reduce rework, and launch services that work for everyone on day one.
